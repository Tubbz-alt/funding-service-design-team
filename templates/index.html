Funding Service Design team

The Funding Service Design team is a new user centred design team based in digital delivery. The team is comprised of five full time civil servants;

  • Product Manager
  • User Researcher
  • Interaction Designer
  • Service Designer
  • Delivery Manager

Our vision

To enable teams to provide funding services that work for all users by delivering a consistent set of tools and standards; which, will provide an increased user experience for external delivery bodies applying for funding as well as for internal users delivering and providing assurance within MHCLG.

What is user centred design (UCD)?

UCD is an iterative design process which focuses on users and their needs in each phase of the design process. In UCD, teams involve users throughout the design process via a variety of research and design methods to create usable, accessible products and services that meet user needs.
